Our Story

Born in Sikar, Built for Gamers

CityByteHub was founded by two passionate partners — one bringing deep technical expertise and the other managing day-to-day operations — with a single shared dream: to create a truly premium gaming and internet experience right here in Sikar.

We wanted Sikar's students and gamers to have access to top-tier hardware, fast internet, proper LAN events, and a great gaming atmosphere — without travelling to big cities. CityByteHub on Piprali Road is our answer.

Whether you're here to grind ranked games, enter a LAN tournament, browse the web, or get printing done — we've got you, every day from 7AM to 9PM.
